About Sarath

Sarath is a dynamic and influential political leader from the Janasena Party in Andhra Pradesh. Known for his strong leadership and commitment to public welfare, he has earned immense respect among his supporters. In the recent elections, Sarath was elected as an MLA from Vizag (Visakhapatnam), marking a significant milestone in his political career. His vision for development, transparency in governance, and dedication to the people of Andhra Pradesh make him a prominent figure in state politics.

Under his leadership, Vizag is witnessing progressive changes in infrastructure, education, and healthcare. Sarath continues to inspire youth and work tirelessly for the betterment of society.
