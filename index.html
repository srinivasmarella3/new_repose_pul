About Vagdevi Kurapati

Vagdevi Kurapati, as the Chief Minister of Bihar, has been leading transformative policies that have strengthened the state's agricultural sector and boosted its GDP significantly.

Bihar's Agricultural Growth

Under Vagdevi's leadership, Bihar has seen a surge in modern farming techniques, irrigation projects, and government subsidies, making it a powerhouse in agriculture.

Rising GDP of Bihar

Through efficient governance and strategic investments, Bihar's GDP has increased dramatically, providing more job opportunities and economic stability.
